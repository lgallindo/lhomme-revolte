# L'Homme Révolté - ELI5 Menu Manual

Welcome to the menu! Think of this menu like a simple TV remote control. 

## How to move around

- **W and S keys (or Up/Down arrows):** These are your "Channel" buttons. Press them to move the glowing box up and down so you can pick what you want to do.
- **Left and Right arrows:** These are your "Volume" buttons. 
  - If you are on **play**, pressing Left or Right changes which level you start on.
  - If you are on **sound**, pressing Left or Right changes the sound settings (like turning music off).

## How to click things

Since there is no mouse pointer to click with, you have to use buttons:
- **Enter key (or J, or Left Mouse Button):** This is your **OK** button. It confirms whatever option you are currently highlighting.
- **Shift key (or K, or Right Mouse Button):** This is your **Back / Cancel** button. Press it to escape the menu and go back to the game.

## What the options do

1. **play:** Starts the game! 
2. **load:** Loads your saved game so you don't lose your progress.
3. **continue:** Closes the menu and puts you back into the action.
4. **map:** Shows you a 2D drawing of the current level.
5. **sound:** Lets you choose if you want Sound Effects, Music, Both, or None.
6. **look:** Flips the mouse movement upside down (for people who play flight simulators).
7. **exit:** Closes the entire game.
