musi kepeken ilo (Controls)

  • tawa (Move): setas tawa, WASD, keyboard nanpa, or Q/E (tawa poka)
  • lukin (Look/Aim): ilo mouse (ilo luka woka pona / Right Click li open/pini e lukin sewi anpa), setas pi poka L/R
  • utala / pona (Shoot/Confirm): mouse left click, [J], [ENTER], [CTRL]
  • tawa poka / pini (Strafe/Cancel): [LSHIFT], [K]
  • sewi (Jump): [SPACE]
  • sitelen ma (Map): [TAB] or [BACKSPACE]
  • lipu open (Menu): [ESC]
  • ante ilo (Change weapon): [F], [O], [P], [X], [Y], [Z], or mouse wheel
