Controls & Keybindings

  • Move: Arrow keys, WASD, numpad, or Q/E (strafe keys)
  • Look / Rotate Camera: Move the mouse or use Left/Right Arrow keys
  • Shoot / Confirm: Mouse Left Click, [J], [ENTER], or [CTRL]
  • Strafe / Cancel: Mouse Right Click or [LSHIFT] / [K]
  • Jump: [SPACE]
  • Map: [TAB] or [BACKSPACE]
  • Menu: [ESC]
  • Change Weapon: [F], [O], [P], [X], [Y], [Z], or mouse wheel

Menu Descriptions

  • Play: Starts a new campaign or resumes the active game.
  • Settings: Adjust sound volume, graphics quality, and dither/minify sprites settings.
  • Look: Adjusts vertical camera look mode: 0 for disabled, 1 for auto-centering look, or 2 for manual look.
  • Credits: Displays information about the original creators and our fork's code.
  • Exit: Exits the game session.
