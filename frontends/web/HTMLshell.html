Menu Descriptions

  • play: Start a new campaign or select a starting level.
  • load: Load your saved game (only available during the current browser session after saving progress in the menu; lost on page reload).
  • sound fx: Toggle sound effects.
  • music: Toggle music.
  • language: Select your preferred language (only available in this multi-language edition).

Controls & Keybindings

  • Move: Arrow keys, WASD, numpad, or Q/E (strafe keys)
  • Look / Rotate Camera: Move the mouse (Right-click to toggle vertical look up/down) or Left/Right arrows
  • Shoot / Confirm: Mouse Left Click, [J], [ENTER], or [CTRL]
  • Strafe / Cancel: [LSHIFT] / [K]
  • Jump: [SPACE]
  • Map: [TAB] or [BACKSPACE]
  • Menu: [ESC]
  • Change Weapon: [F], [O], [P], [X], [Y], [Z], or mouse wheel
