musi kepeken ilo (Controls)

  • tawa (Move): setas tawa, WASD, keyboard nanpa, or Q/E (tawa poka)
  • lukin (Look/Aim): ilo mouse, setas pi poka L/R
  • utala / pona (Shoot/Confirm): mouse left click, [J], [ENTER], [CTRL]
  • tawa poka / pini (Strafe/Cancel): mouse right click, [LSHIFT], [K]
  • sewi (Jump): [SPACE]
  • sitelen ma (Map): [TAB] or [BACKSPACE]
  • lipu open (Menu): [ESC]
  • ante ilo (Change weapon): [F], [O], [P], [X], [Y], [Z], or mouse wheel

lipu open pi nasin musi (Menus)

  • open (Play): o open e musi sin.
  • ante (Settings): o ante e kalama e sitelen musi.
  • lukin (Look): o ante e nasin lukin pi sinpin sewi anpa. 0 li pini lukin, 1 li lukin pi ante sewi (pona), 2 li lukin pi ante sewi (pini lukin la ilo li tawa insa).
  • lipu pona (Credits): o lukin e jan pi musi ni.
  • pini (Exit): o pini e musi.
